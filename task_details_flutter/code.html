Task Description

Join our mission to empower the next generation. This task involves conducting a two-hour virtual or in-person workshop focused on basic arithmetic and problem-solving for children aged 8-12. Your role is to make learning fun through interactive games and relatable examples.

4

Submit the completion report and photos within 24 hours of finishing.
